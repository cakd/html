Most common flag colors

Posted by James Gurney on 2013-07-04T08:30:00.001-04:00
Steve Faeembra created this infographic to show which international flag colors are the most popular.

Red, white, and blue and not only the most popular individual colors, but they're also the most popular colors in combination with each other. According to Wikipedia, there are over 30 countries and territories with red, white, and blue flags:
