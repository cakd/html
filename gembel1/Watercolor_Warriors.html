Watercolor Warriors

Posted by James Gurney on 2012-08-06T01:42:00.000-04:00

(Direct link to video) Painting outdoors with watercolor is a tough challenge, even for highly trained professionals. In this episode Jeanette and I square off against dangerous wasps, rain, and wind in an attempt to paint an abandoned farm tractor.

Tools: Schmincke Watercolor Pocket Set , Caran D'Ache watercolor pencils, Moleskine Watercolor Notebook, and a 1/4 inch flat watercolor travel brush, Matthews Century Stand , Artist Umbrella and a fridge super magnet (thanks, Steve), shot on a Canon VIXIA.
